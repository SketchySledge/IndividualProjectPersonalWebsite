Interests and Hobbies

My Podcast

I am one of the hosts of the EarBudz podcast on Spotify. While it is currently just something to play around with, my co-host and I hope to gain real footing in the podcast world and make something of it.

Filmmaking

I found an interest in editing and creating films in 2018. Since then, I have involved myself in numerous ways of filmmaking through classes, organizations, and competitions. I sincerely enjoy this art medium and hope to one day make a career out of it.

My Art

I have been drawing for as long as I can remember. I really started trying in roughly 2013. I made an Instagram account as a gallery for all of my work and I post most of my bigger artwork there. Through these practices, I have been commissioned to illustrate a book. I hope to maintain this hobby throughout my life.

Dungeons and Dragons

At age 15, I found a new love of the table-top RPG Dungeons and Dragons. It has become a routine part of my life and brought me closer to many people, as well as brought me to find some new friends. Dungeons and Dragons has not only helped provide me with entertainment in a creative outlet, but also provided me with new skills in terms of communication and improvisation.

Heelys

People always tell me to accomplish my dreams and shoot for the stars. Well recently, I bought myself a pair of Heelys and my life was changed forever. This mode of transportation has proven revolutionary in my life and I truly cannot fathom how I lived without it. As an asthmatic, Heelys have helped me to walk less, thus not have to breathe as hard. Lung problems aside, this footwear has gained me the attention of many, going so far as to help win over my current girlfriend. The convenience of Heelys is unmatched and I would suggest them to anyone in question.
